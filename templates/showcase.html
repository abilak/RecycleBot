Discover Unique Eco-Friendly Companies

Explore these innovative brands making a difference in the world!

Allbirds

Allbirds creates sustainable shoes using materials like wool, eucalyptus, and sugarcane. They aim to reduce their carbon footprint and have pioneered eco-conscious footwear design.

MiiR

MiiR creates sustainable drinkware and bags. Each product sold supports giving projects around the world related to clean water, healthy environments, and community-building.

Eir NYC

Eir NYC offers all-natural skincare products using organic ingredients with minimal packaging. Their mission is to create effective, sustainable skincare products with a focus on environmental stewardship.

Trashie

Trashie designs fashionable accessories made entirely from upcycled waste materials. Their mission is to reduce landfill waste while promoting creativity through innovative, sustainable fashion.

ECOALF

ECOALF creates sustainable clothing and accessories from recycled materials, such as plastic bottles and discarded fishing nets. Their mission is to demonstrate that fashion can be both stylish and sustainable.

Bureo

Bureo transforms discarded fishing nets into skateboards, sunglasses, and other products. By repurposing waste, they help reduce ocean plastic pollution and support sustainable design.

Pela

Pela creates compostable phone cases, reducing the environmental impact of phone accessories. Their products are designed to break down without leaving harmful waste behind.
