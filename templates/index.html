Mission

RecycleBot is here to help you make eco-friendly choices. A major issue is knowing which items go in which bins. Many recyclable items end up in the trash, or worse, trash goes in the recycling bin. This harms the environment and creates extra work for recycling centers.

How to Use

  • Take a picture in the "Classify" section to get an instant classification of your item (recyclable, compostable, or trash).
  • Upload images to help us improve our model and increase its accuracy over time.
  • Take or upload multiple pictures, and get classifications for each one.
  • Contribute to the research by submitting images to improve our model and help us make better predictions.
  • Explore the "Eco-Friendly Businesses" section to discover sustainable companies you can support.
  • Find Recycling Centers & Food Charities near you to be more aware of environmentally-friendly services near you.

Support Eco-Friendly Businesses

  • Find businesses that focus on sustainable practices.
  • Support companies that minimize waste and use recyclable materials.
  • Help reduce carbon footprints by making informed choices.
